About Us

Grand Tycoon Enterprise LLC is based in Shreveport, LA and focuses on buying, refurbishing, and building affordable rental homes. We aim to transform the Arklatex region—Northwest Louisiana, East Texas, and South Arkansas—into thriving, safe, and family-oriented communities.

Our Projects

Our flagship initiative involves revitalizing the Tulsa District in the Cedar Grove neighborhood with 20–35 shotgun-style duplex rental homes (1200–1500 sq ft per side), alongside local businesses like a butcher shop, mechanic garage, and boutique—all tailored for long-term community benefit.
